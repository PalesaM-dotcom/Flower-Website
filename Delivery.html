Delivery Information

At Florea, we want your flowers to arrive fresh and beautiful, right on time. Here’s everything you need to know about our delivery process.

1. Delivery Areas

  • Johannesburg, Pretoria, and surrounding areas

2. Delivery Times

  • Same-Day Delivery: Orders placed before 10 AM are eligible.
  • Next-Day Delivery: Orders placed after 10 AM will be delivered the next business day.
  • Scheduled Delivery: Choose a specific date up to 30 days in advance.
Note: Deliveries are only made on business days (Monday to Saturday).

3. Delivery Fees

  • Orders over R2500: Free delivery
  • Orders under R2500: Depends on location
  • Remote areas: Additional charges may apply (we’ll confirm before delivery)

4. How to Ensure Successful Delivery

  • Provide accurate delivery details (address, apartment number, gate codes, etc.)
  • Include a contact number for the recipient
  • Make sure someone is available to receive the flowers

5. Handling Delays or Issues

We take extra care to ensure your flowers arrive on time. In case of unexpected delays, our customer support team will contact you immediately.

6. Gift Messages

You can include a short note with your delivery. We’ll print it on a beautiful card and deliver it with your flowers.
